About Our College

  • The Modern Education Society's College of Engineering, Pune was established in 1999.
  • It conducts the following courses leading to Bachelor's Degree in Engineering & Master Degree in Engineering..
    1. - Computer Engineering
    2. - Mechanical Engineering
    3. - Electronics and Tele communication
    4. - AR Engineering

Our Goals and Objectives

  • 1. To ensure all-round development of students by providing co-curricular, extra-curricular and entrepreneurship skills.
  • 2. To imbibe research culture in students and faculty by providing state-of-the-art research facilities and Industry Institute interaction.
  • 3. To inculcate moral integrity in students and make them socially responsible and good human being.
  • 4.To develop leadership qualities and the ability to work as a team among students by encouraging participation in various activities..

Mission

  • 1. Promoting Value Education through motivated trained faculty.
  • 2. Maintaining conducive environment for education at affordable cost.
  • 3. Promoting Industry Institute interaction.
  • 4. Involving Alumni.

About Traning and Placement Cell

Welcome to the Training and Placement (T&P) Cell. MESCOE's T&P Cell ensures that some of the best organizations visit the campus on a regular basis to offer employment to the students for work in the areas of Computer, Electronics & Telecommunication and Mechanical Engineering domains."

Over the years, the T&P cell has maintained a good and strong relationship with Industries across the country and as a result, has built up an impressive placement record both in terms of the percentage of students placed, as well as the number of companies visiting the campus. The T&P Cell organizes various training & personality development programs to ensure that the students are industry-ready. It also assists the departments in organizing Technical Workshops, Guest Lectures and factory/plant visits for its students to some major industries in and around Pune. We truly appreciate the faith bestowed on us by several esteemed organizations and look forward to continuing our relationship with them for building a better future for our engineers."
